INYF’ Children Festival

"INYF’ NATIONAL CHILDREN’S FESTIVAL LIVE in COLOUR!"

We are a place that family and friends can rely on to gather, share, laugh and make memories. With that spirit in mind, The INYF is proud to present the 2015 National Children’s Festival. By offering quality experiences to the youngest members of our community, we are investing in the future and helping little minds to grow, blossom and experience things LIVE in COLOUR. This vibrant Festival encourages children to explore and experience different cultures, traditions and means of artistic expression. By challenging imaginations and fulfilling curiosities, we are helping kids gain confidence in their abilities, take pride in their accomplishments, and cultivate a positive view of the world around them. When it comes to our children, they see things LIVE in COLOUR. Youngsters see the world with vibrant, positive energy where anything and everything is possible. By watching a performance, looking at others’ artwork or making a new discovery, they are igniting that inner spark of creativity and curiosity that lasts a lifetime. Children don’t view the world in black and white – they live and dream in a world of colour. It allows them to reach for the stars and realize their full potential. Events like the INYF National Children’s Festival provide kids with the brightest and boldest colours possible to paint their own paths in life. LIVE in COLOUR, children can sing and dance to world music, interact with roving artists, listen to stories about faraway places, and create their own masterpieces with the help of a multitude of hands-on activities. Performances and activities are meticulously planned to introduce kids to a cultural kaleidoscope of sights, sounds, and one-of-a-kind experiences such as puppetry, music, dance, theatre, clowning, acrobatics, literary and visual arts. “When you reduce life to black and white, you’ll never see rainbows.” (Rachel Houston)
